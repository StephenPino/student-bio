Student Bio

Stephen Pino

Stephen Matthew Pino was born on July 25, 1993 in Dover, Delaware. His mother, Donna Pino, was a special education teacher and his father, Anthony Pino, was a pilot in the United States Airforce. Stephen moved to San Antonio Texas when he was 9 weeks old, where he would spend the next 18 years of his life. After graduating high school, Stephen attended the University of Texas at San Antonio for one year as an undeclared student. He then transferred to the University of Texas at Austin where he would spend the next 4 years obtaining his bachelor's of science and arts in Neuroscience. Stephen is currently enrolled in The Coding Bootcamp program at the University of Texas at Austin where he hopes to build a career as a fullstack developer.
